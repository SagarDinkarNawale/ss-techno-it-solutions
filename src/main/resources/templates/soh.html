SOH (Setup, Operate, Handover) Model

A Turnkey Solution for Streamlined GCC implementation

The SOH model is designed to simplify and accelerate your initiatives. We take complete ownership of your project, from the initial setup and configuration to ongoing operation and eventual handover to your internal team. This comprehensive approach ensures a smooth and successful implementation, minimizing risk and maximizing efficiency.

Benefits of the SOH Model

Reduced Time to Value

Our experienced team can quickly get your system up and running, allowing you to reap the benefits of your IT investment sooner

Minimized Risk

We handle all aspects of the project, mitigating risks associated with implementation and configuratio

Expert Knowledge Transfer

Throughout the process, we share our expertise with your designated internal team, ensuring a smooth knowledge handover and long-term system management capabilities.

Reduced Resource Burden

Free up your internal IT resources to focus on core business activities while we handle the implementation and initial

Cost-Effective Solution

The SOH model provides a predictable cost structure, eliminating hidden expenses associated with in-house implementation.


The SOH Process

Planning and Analysis

We work closely with you to understand your specific needs, define project scope, and develop a
detailed implementation plan.

System Setup and Configuration

Our team takes care of all setup and configuration tasks, ensuring the system aligns perfectly with your requirements.

Ongoing Operation and Management

We provide comprehensive operation and management services, including user support, system maintenance, and performance optimization.

Knowledge Transfer and Handover

Throughout the project, we share our expertise with your internal team, ensuring a seamless handover process and long-term system management capabilities.

By choosing the SOH model, you gain peace of mind knowing you are in the hands of experienced professionals. Aikya Technologies streamlines the entire implementation process, minimizes risk, and ensures a successful handover to your team, empowering you to unlock the full potential of your new IT solution.
